<h2>Staff Recognition</h2>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Staff_Recognition_2025.png">
</div>
<p>We are proud to share that a group of our teachers have been nominated
for the President’s Award for Teachers (PAT) 2025 and the Outstanding Youth
in Education Award (OYEA) 2025 – two of the most prestigious national accolades
that honour exemplary educators in Singapore.</p>
<p>These nominations are a recognition of their unwavering dedication, innovative
teaching practices, and their deep commitment to nurturing every pupil
to be a confident learner and contributing member of society. Beyond the
classroom, they inspire others through professional sharing, mentoring,
and collaborative leadership, embodying the true spirit of the teaching
profession.</p>
<p></p>
<h3><strong>Staffs' Achievements</strong></h3>

<p><strong>The MOE Service Excellence Award (MSEA)</strong>
</p>
<p>We are proud to share that staff from Si Ling Primary School are recipients
of the prestigious MOE Service Excellence Award (MSEA), which recognises
MOE staff who demonstrate passion in service delivery, consistently exemplify
C.A.R.E. (<em>Collaborate, Anticipate, Reach out, Empathise</em>) and inspire
others to do the same.</p>
