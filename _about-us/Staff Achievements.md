<p>The MOE Service Excellence Award (MSEA)
<br>
<br>The MOE Service Excellence Award (MSEA) is an MOE-wide award that recognises
MOE staff who are passionate in their service delivery, consistently serve
internal and external customers with C.A.R.E (Collaborate, Anticipate,
Reach out, Empathise) and inspire their colleagues to do likewise.</p>
